Prices
For a guide to what your cake might cost please browse my portfolio to see some completed cakes. Or for a quote please contact me directly.

Celebration cake prices

Celebration cakes start at £40 for a plain iced 7 inch round sponge cake (£50 for rich fruit cake). Prices reflect cake size and number and complexity of decorations. Sculpted cakes will usually cost considerably more due to factors like cake wastage, cost of internal supports and time spent carving.

Wedding cake prices

Two tier cakes from: £120
Three tier cakes from: £200
Four tier cakes from: £290
Five tier cakes from: £390
Personalised bride and groom toppers from: £50

For an additional cost I can set up the cake at your wedding venue if necessary. Please ask for a quote.

Cookie prices (minimum order 24)

Cookies range from £2—£3.50 each, depending on complexity of design. Each cookie comes individually wrapped in a clear cellophane bag and tied with a pretty satin ribbon.

Cupcake prices (minimum order 12)

Basic cupcakes start at £1.50 each and then additional costs for decorations.

Discounts

Discounts can be negotiated on large orders (i.e. 50+) for both cookies and cupcakes.

Flavours
